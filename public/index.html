BuyMoreWine!

I was shopping for wine the other day and it occured to me that finding wines now is not that different than finding books and music was before Amazon and Apple got to it. It is really difficult to find stuff. I also noticed that no one in the wine retail has any clue how to search for anything. This site is solely dedicated to making search for wine as well as store that has it easy. It starts really small but it'll grow and grow and grow some more. Make no mistake, as any self-respecting tech startup megalomaniacs we are bent on world domination. Lastly Robert Parker doesn't live here and neither does Two Buck Chuck...
